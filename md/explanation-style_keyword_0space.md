This rule checks the whitespace immediately following these keywords:
`break`
, `continue`
, `default`
, `new`
, `null`
, `super`
, and `this`.
Uses of these keywords should never have any whitespace between the keyword and
the following symbol, e.g.
`break;`,
, `continue;`
, `default:`
, `new[5]`
, `(myexample == null)`
, or `super.foo`.
